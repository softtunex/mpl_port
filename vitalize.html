Project Overview

Vitalyze is a proactive customer management platform designed to help businesses monitor and improve customer health. My goal was to create an intuitive dashboard that uses data-driven insights to identify at-risk accounts, forecast potential churn, and empower teams to take timely, strategic action, ultimately boosting long-term customer satisfaction and loyalty.

Duration

2 weeks

Role

UX Designer, UI Designer

Tools used

Figma, figjam

Challenge & Solutions

The problems it solve

There are many things related to customer management that can be solved with vitalize.

PROBLEM 01

Customer success teams are often reactive, addressing issues only after they arise, which leads to inefficiencies and customer churn.

SOLUTION

From Reactive to Proactive

Vitalyze solves this by providing a proactive, AI-driven dashboard that monitors customer data and automatically flags at-risk accounts, enabling teams to intervene before a problem escalates.

PROBLEM 02

CRMs often provide raw data, not actionable insights. This makes it difficult for teams to prioritize and focus on the most critical accounts.

SOLUTION

Actionable Insights Dashboard

Vitalyze provides a clear customer health score that transforms complex data into actionable insights, allowing teams to instantly see and focus on at-risk accounts.

PROBLEM 03

CSMs spend too much time on manual, repetitive tasks like data entry. This takes away from time that could be spent on meaningful customer engagement.

SOLUTION

From Reactive to Proactive

By automating administrative tasks, Vitalyze enables CSMs to focus on impactful conversations and building relationships.
