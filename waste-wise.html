Project overview

Waste Wise is a mobile application designed to address the complexities of waste management in urban areas. The goal was to create a simple, intuitive, and engaging platform that helps users properly sort their waste, schedule pickups, and track their recycling contributions. The app aims to empower individuals to make a positive environmental impact by demystifying waste disposal processes and providing a personalized way to measure their progress.

Duration

2 weeks

Role

UX Designer, UI Designer

Tools used

Figma, figjam

Problem statement

Urban residents often resort to improper waste disposal due to a lack of clear sorting guidelines and convenient pickup management. They struggle to know what's recyclable, miss collection days, and lack a clear way to track their positive environmental impact. This confusion leads to widespread waste contamination and frustration for those who want to be more environmentally responsible.

Project Goal

The key objectives for this project was to increase user participation in proper waste sorting, reduce waste contamination, and create a user-friendly way for residents to manage their recyclable and non-recyclable waste. The app needed to be a comprehensive tool that simplifies the waste management process from start to finish.

User Research

An online survey consisting of 20 participants was conducted from the primary user group to identify similarity and common pattern between users.

Research Questions

  • How do you currently dispose of your waste at home?
  • Do you have a specific method for disposing of hazardous waste? If Yes specify
  • What is your current recycling frequency?
  • What are the main challenges you face when it comes to proper waste disposal and recycling?
  • How do these challenges impact your daily life?
  • Do you believe that younger generations will play a significant role in changing waste disposal practices and recycling systems through apps?
  • What is your current waste Disposal Frequency?
  • Do you find sorting waste a difficult task?
  • Can you mention few steps through which you could help you manage waste better?

Solution

Home page

This feature allows a user to identify and categorize an item of waste. The user can either take a new picture or upload one from their library. The app then identifies the item and its category, providing clarity on how to dispose of it.

Schedule Pickup

A seamless pickup flow, where users after selecting their waste item, choose a convenient date and time, confirm their address - followed by a real time status tracking.
