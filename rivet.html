Project timeline

Rivet aims to empower individuals with a comprehensive, secure, and user-friendly financial platform that simplifies money management, enhances financial literacy, and provides access to global financial opportunities.

Rivet goes beyond basic wallet functionalities by offering a suite of advanced features tailored to meet diverse financial needs. Users can effortlessly convert currencies within the app ensuring they get the basic exchange rates for their transactions.

Transaction

Fundz Transfer

Flyfundz facilitates sending and receiving money to and from international banks, making global transactions straightforward and hassle-free.

Making local and international transaction easier

Upon registration, users receive a virtual Naira card for immediate use in local transactions, ensuring seamless integration into the Nigerian financial ecosystem.

After completing additional registration steps, users can obtain a virtual dollar card, allowing them to make purchases and transact in foreign markets effortlessly.

Instant Dollar/Naira card

Upon registration, user receive a virtual Naira & Dollar card for immediate use in local & international transactions, ensuring seamless integration into the financial ecosystem.

Currency Conversion

Flyfundz offers real-time currency conversion within the app, enabling users to switch between Naira and Dollar with competitive exchange rates.

Stock Purchases

The app includes a feature for buying company stocks, giving users the opportunity to invest in the stock market directly from their mobile devices.

Saving Targets

Users can create and manage saving targets within the app, helping them save systematically for specific goals, such as vacations, education, etc.
